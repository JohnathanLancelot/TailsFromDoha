Tails From Doha

Cheshire

In Doha Qatar, it is tragically common for expats to abandon their cats when moving back home after a work project. We were informed that this gorgeous dollface Persian - who looked so out of place among the strays - was one such case. Cheshire - later renamed 'Autumn' by her forever family - was not only pregnant with Hugo and Duma when we met her, but was still sticking close to her feral-born, pregant daughters Montessa and Bandit. She groomed and even allowed Montessa to suckle from her when they both were pregant! Cheshire was a dedicated mother and one of the sweetest cats I've ever met.

After getting her desexed, once her kittens were weaned, and vaccinated, microchipped, worm and flea treated, we were very selective in finding her the perfect home - one that loves her for the wonderful kitty she is, rather than seeing her as a status symbol. She is now living a life of luxury in her new and loving home!
